Guidance & Information

Types of Waste and Disposal Methods

  • Plastic: Recycle plastics by segregating them into PET, HDPE, and others. Avoid single-use plastics.
  • Paper: Recycle paper products like newspapers, cardboard, and office paper. Reduce usage by opting for digital alternatives.
  • Organic: Compost organic waste such as food scraps and yard waste. Use the compost in gardening.
  • Glass: Recycle glass bottles and jars. Separate by color if required by local recycling programs.
  • Metal: Recycle metal cans, foil, and scrap metal. Rinse and clean them before disposal.

Guidelines for Managing Waste Efficiently

  • Reduce: Minimize waste generation by choosing reusable products and buying in bulk.
  • Reuse: Repurpose items instead of discarding them. Donate usable items to those in need.
  • Recycle: Sort waste into recyclable categories and follow local recycling guidelines.
  • Compost: Convert organic waste into valuable compost for gardening and agriculture.
  • Educate: Spread awareness about waste management practices in your community.
